intro

Hi, I'm Marie! I am a Psychology student at NYU graduating this May 2025. I started doing research my sophomore year studying facial perception and mental state inferences. In my position, I got to analyze data using Jamovi, set up online experiments on Qualtrics, and code little programs such as one that randomly assigns participants to different conditions.

Nowadays, I am working in Todd Gureckis' Lab in Computation & Cognition to understand problems of insight. Our current project involves the New York Times Connections game and compares the playing experience of humans and classic NLP models. Through our research, we hope to learn if, and how, we may be able to generate experiences of insight through the manipulation of game difficulty.
