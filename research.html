intro

Hi, I'm Marie! I am currently in my final year at NYU and will be receiving my undergraduate degree in Psychology and Computer Science this coming May 2025. Though it took me nearly the entirety of my undergrad to figure out what I wanted to pair with my psychology degree (I have taken courses in global public health, premed, child and adolescent mental health, and finally computer science), I am happy to declare my intention in pursuing research in computational cognitive science. This field, with all of its exciting potential research avenues, combines my two passions of psychology and computer science to better understand human cognition via the development of computer models and simulations that seek to emulate human behavior.

Currently, I am working in Todd Gureckis' Lab in Computation & Cognition with Solim Legris to understand problems of insight. When I'm not in the lab, I enjoy reading, listening to music, going for walks in the city, and trying out new restaurants with my friends. I've recently taken up web design, too!
